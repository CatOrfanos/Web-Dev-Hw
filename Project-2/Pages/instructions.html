COOKING INGREDIENTS AND INFO:

  1. 1 hour, plus chilling and cooling times. Makes about 3 dozen brownies.
  2. 1 ½ cups (3 sticks) butter
  3. 2 cups (14 oz) granulated sugar
  4. 1 1/3 cups (4 ¼ oz) cocoa
  5. 2 oz cream cheese, cut into small pieces
  6. 4 eggs
  7. ½ tsp salt
  8. 1 cup (4 ¼ oz) flour
  9. 8 oz chopped bittersweet chocolate, divided
  10. 1 1/3 cups coarsely chopped toasted walnuts (optional)
  11. 2 tbls powdered sugar
  12. ¼ cup heavy cream, warmed

Its always great to source many different recipes to find the best one for you. Dorie Greenspan's Baking: From My Home to Yours has been recomended to us many times! After this recipe you should check it out!

FINALLY:

  1. Heat Oven to 325 degrees
  2. Mix all your ingredients together in a bowl
  3. Poor into 13 by 9 inch baking pan
  4. Let cook for 35 to 45 min
  5. Let cool for 15 min or until cool
  6. Poor a glass of milk
  7. ENJOY!
