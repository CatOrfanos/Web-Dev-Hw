PROJECT SUMARY:

Today we are going to learn how to make the BEST BROWNIES EVER! 1st We will start by learning what ingredients we need to start the brownie making process! COOKING INGREDIENTS AND INSTRUCTIONS:

  1. 1 hour, plus chilling and cooling times. Makes about 3 dozen brownies.
  2. 1 ½ cups (3 sticks) butter
  3. 2 cups (14 ounces) granulated sugar
  4. 1 1/3 cups (4 ¼ ounces) cocoa
  5. 2 ounces cream cheese, cut into small pieces
  6. 4 eggs
  7. ½ teaspoon salt
  8. 1 cup (4 ¼ ounces) flour
  9. 8 ounces chopped bittersweet chocolate, divided
  10. 1 1/3 cups coarsely chopped toasted walnuts (optional)
  11. 2 tablespoons powdered sugar
  12. ¼ cup heavy cream, warmed
